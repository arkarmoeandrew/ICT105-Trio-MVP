# Assumption-Evidence Table

| No. | Team Assumption | Evidence Collected | Evidence Type | Strength | Decision |
|------|----------------|-------------------|--------------|----------|----------|
| 1 | Students frequently need temporary access to academic resources and equipment. | Most respondents reported needing calculators, textbooks, cameras, chargers, or project equipment. | Interview pattern | Strong | Keep |

| 4 | Students would be willing to share resources and skills through a trusted platform. | Many respondents said they would lend items or offer help if users were verified. | Interview pattern | Moderate | Keep |
| 5 | A university-exclusive platform would improve trust. | Most respondents preferred RSU email verification and student-only access. | Interview pattern | Strong | Keep |
| 6 | Students need both resource-sharing and skill-sharing features. | Respondents mentioned tutoring, programming help, video editing, and equipment borrowing. | Interview pattern | Strong | Keep |
| 7 | Every student will actively use the platform. | Some students said they would only use it when facing urgent needs. | Interview pattern | Weak/Moderate | Revise |
